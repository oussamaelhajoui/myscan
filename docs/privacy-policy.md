`myscan` does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties.

## 3. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data.

Specifically, we do not collect:

- Name, email, phone number, address, identifiers, or account data

## 4. Local Network Functionality

`myscan` requests local network access only to perform user-initiated network scanning on networks you choose.

Scan activity and results are used for in-app functionality on your device. No scan results are transmitted to the developer.

## 5. Legal Bases (EU/EEA/UK GDPR)
